Skills & Experiences

A software developer is a skilled professional who specializes in creating, designing, and maintaining computer programs and applications. They are proficient in various programming languages and frameworks, working on projects that cater to specific user needs and business requirements. Their expertise spans across different areas, including front-end development, back-end development, and database management. On the other hand, a full-stack developer is a versatile expert who possesses a comprehensive understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies. They are capable of handling all aspects of the development process, from user interface design and user experience to server-side programming and database management. Full-stack developers have the ability to navigate the entire development stack, making them valuable assets in building robust, end-to-end solutions for modern web and software applications.
